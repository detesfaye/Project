Stories from Around the World - Ethiopia

Ethiopia is an east African country that has a diverse culture and tradition. Until 1974 Ethiopia was ruled by the Monarch. More than 80 languages are spoken in Ethiopia. It has nine regions with each region having its own leader. Ethiopia is the birthplace of Coffe! Ethiopia also has its own calendar and time.
